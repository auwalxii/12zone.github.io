12zone is an Information Technology Consultancy Firm and Training Academy. Our main focus is on Data Analytics, Web Applications and Block-Chain Technology. The Compnay was founded in the year 2012 with the vision of providing Web Hosting Services but re-directed it is services to Software Development, Training and other IT related services.

It is a well-known fact that, thausands of young men and women graduated every year from tertiary institutions across Nigeria with various qualifications from different disciplines, but most of them lack the basic skills needed to successfully put what ever they learn into practice to secure or build better economic opportunities.

This company serves as an IT Hub for students and graduates of Computer Science and other related discipline. We provide them with the resources and guidence they need to learn, research, practice, and transform thier ideas into real world applications. Finnaly we guide the successful once on securing an investment to futher develop their Products or Services into successful businesses.
